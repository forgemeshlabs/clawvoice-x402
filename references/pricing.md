# Pricing

Default package posture:

- Free ClawHub skill install.
- Free local wallet creation.
- Free local Supertonic installer helper.
- ForgeMesh fee: `$0.005` per successful paid x402 call where ForgeMesh provides hosted voice fallback, compatibility routing, receipt logging, payment normalization, or discovery assistance.

Do not describe the `$0.005` as the total cost of every downstream service. It is a ForgeMesh layer fee. The downstream x402 product may charge its own price.

Recommended public wording:

> Local voice is free after setup. Hosted ForgeMesh/x402 calls add a `$0.005` ForgeMesh compatibility fee plus the downstream endpoint price.
